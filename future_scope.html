Potential Enhancements and Future Development Directions

Severity Classification

Implement graduated severity levels to prioritize response and enable appropriate action based on threat level.

Severity Levels

  • Level 1 - Mild: Minor insults, general negativity
  • Level 2 - Moderate: Personal attacks, repeated harassment
  • Level 3 - Severe: Threats, hate speech, doxxing
  • Level 4 - Critical: Credible threats, emergency intervention needed

Response Actions

  • Automated warnings for mild cases
  • Content removal for moderate cases
  • Account suspension for severe cases
  • Emergency services notification for critical cases
